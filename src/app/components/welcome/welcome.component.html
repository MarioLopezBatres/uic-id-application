Required documents

In order to proceed, you will need the following documentation:

1. Original/full Birth Certificate

2. Original Citizenship Certificate (Only People born outside Namibia)

3. Marriage Certificate (Married Women only)

4. Original Permanent Resident Permit (Permanent Residents only)

5. Old Namibian ID (Only applicants in possession of an old ID)

6. ID Photo
